FAQs: Answers to your catering questions

  • What types of cuisines do you offer for catering?

    We serve a wide variety of cuisines, including authentic Indian, Thai, Chinese, Mexican, Italian, and European favourites. Our menu is tailored to your preferences and the theme of your event.

  • Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and special requests?

    Absolutely. We understand the importance of accommodating dietary preferences and restrictions. Our chefs are skilled at customizing dishes to meet your specific needs, whether it's vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or any other dietary requirement.

  • How can I request a quote for catering services?

    You can request a quote by contacting our team through our website or by giving us a call. We'll be happy to discuss your event details, menu options, and provide you with a personalized quote.

  • What is the minimum and maximum guest count you can cater to?

    We can cater to events of various sizes, from intimate gatherings to large-scale celebrations. Our flexible approach allows us to accommodate both small and large guest counts, ensuring a seamless catering experience.

  • What sets Shubham Catering apart from other caterers in Bangalore?

    Shubham Catering is distinguished by its commitment to culinary excellence, using only the freshest ingredients, expert chefs, and impeccable presentation. Our dedication to creating unforgettable dining experiences and personalized service sets us apart as your premier choice for catering in Bangalore.
